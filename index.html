Natural, By Design

What we put on your skin

Every fiber is chosen for how it ages, not just how it photographs on day one.

  • GOTS-certified cotton

    Grown without synthetic pesticides, traced from farm to finished garment.

  • Low-impact dyeing

    Water-saving dye houses that use up to 70% less water than conventional mills.

  • Repairable by design

    Reinforced seams and spare buttons included — because we'd rather mend a piece than see it thrown away.
